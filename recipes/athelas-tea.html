Aragorn's Athelas Tea Recipe

Description

Serve to any Man, Hobbit, or Dwarf (Elves don’t get sick, remember?) suffering from congestion, sore throat, dry cough or Morgul blades.

Ingredients

  • Juice of 2 lemons
  • 3-4 1/2 inch coins of fresh Ginger
  • 1 clove of garlic (“You *must* trust Master..”)
  • 3 sprigs of Mint (approx. 7-10 leaves)
  • 2L. Filtered water, boiling
  • Unpasteurised Honey
  • Echinaccia extract, liquid

Instructions

  1. Place Ginger, Mint and Garlic(peeled, but not crushed!) in a cheesecloth and tie off.
  2. Add to boiling water and cook for 5-7 min.
  3. Turn off heat and add lemon juice.
  4. Remove spices and transfer to an insulated tea pot.
  5. Into each cup, add a tsp. of honey(or to taste), and echinaccia extract to the bottle’s instructions.
